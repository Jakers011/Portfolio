Keep the Beat Away Game

Web App Development Coursework

Click here to play

This game was designed to be a fresh concept that relied heavily on music, resulting in a game with immense variance. The game utilizes the P5.js library for beat detection: on a beat an obstacle will spawn and then move with varying speed dependent on the amplitude of the song at that moment. Players must avoid colliding with the obstacles otherwise they will lose lives and eventually the game, with collision and animation based off of P5. Players increase their score by living past a beat and get points based on how many obstacles are on the screen, meaning that songs with higher tempo and higher beats per minute (bpm) will be more challenging and rewarding, and it is up to the player to find their optimal song.
